How to find your current location?

TellMyLocation.com is a premium utility designed for speed. By utilizing the W3C Geolocation API and OpenStreetMap data, we provide users with high-accuracy coordinates in seconds. Whether you need to find your latitude and longitude for a professional project or you're simply asking "where am I right now?", our tool provides the most reliable data without tracking your personal identity.


Is it safe to share my location?

Our tool is built with a "Privacy-First" architecture. Unlike other apps, we do not require downloads or account creation. Your browser asks for permission to access your GPS; if you grant it, the data is shown to you and you alone. We use secure HTTPS encryption to ensure your session is private.
