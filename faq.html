Frequently Asked Questions

This FAQ page answers the most common questions about TellMyLocation.com and how it works.

1. What does TellMyLocation.com do?

The site shows your real-time location, address, weather, altitude, IP address, and nearby places using trusted APIs. Everything loads instantly and works directly in your browser.

2. Do you store my location?

No. Your location is processed locally on your device and never stored, saved, or logged by us.

3. Why do I need to allow location access?

Your browser requires permission before sharing your GPS coordinates. Without permission, we cannot show your exact location or nearby places.

4. What if I deny location access?

The site will still load, but features like your exact location, address, and nearby places will not work.

5. How accurate is the location?

Accuracy depends on your device, browser, and GPS signal. Phones are usually very accurate. Laptops may vary based on Wi-Fi positioning.

6. What APIs or services do you use?

We use trusted providers including OpenStreetMap, Nominatim, Open‑Meteo, Open‑Elevation, Overpass API, ipapi.co, and Leaflet.js.

7. Do you track or sell my data?

No. We do not track, store, sell, or share any personal information. All data is processed in real time and disappears when you leave the site.

8. Why do you show my IP address?

Your IP address helps display your ISP and general network information. It is not stored or linked to any profile.

9. Why are some nearby places missing?

Nearby places come from OpenStreetMap. If a location is missing, it may not be listed in their database yet.

10. Does the site work worldwide?

Yes. The site works globally as long as your device supports location access and you have an internet connection.

11. Is TellMyLocation.com free?

Yes. The site is completely free to use. Optional ads or donations help support the project.
