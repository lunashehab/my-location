Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is TellMyLocation.com really free?

Yes, completely. Our tool is 100% free to use for finding your GPS coordinates, current address, and local weather. We support the site via unobtrusive advertisements.

2. Is my location data private?

Yes. Privacy is our #1 priority. Your location data is processed locally on your device (client-side). We do not store your history, and we do not track your movements. When you close the tab, the data is gone.

3. How accurate is the GPS tool?

The accuracy depends on your device hardware. On a smartphone with GPS enabled, accuracy is usually within 5 to 10 meters. On a desktop computer relying on Wi-Fi triangulation, accuracy may be lower (approx. 50-100 meters).

4. Does this work offline?

You need an internet connection to load the map tiles and address data. However, if your device has a GPS chip, you may still be able to see your raw Latitude/Longitude coordinates even without an active internet connection.

5. Why is the address slightly different from where I am standing?

We use OpenStreetMap data to convert coordinates into an address (Reverse Geocoding). Sometimes, the GPS point lands in the middle of a street or a large building, so the system estimates the nearest registered street number.

6. Can I share my location in an emergency?

Yes. Use the red "SOS Share" button on the homepage. It will generate a link that opens instantly in Google Maps, which you can send via SMS, WhatsApp, or Email.
