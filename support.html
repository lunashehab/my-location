Support

Need help with TellMyLocation.com? You’re in the right place. Our goal is to make sure the site works smoothly for you, no matter where you are or what device you’re using.

1. Common Issues

Location Not Loading

If your location isn’t showing:

  • Make sure you allowed location access in your browser
  • Refresh the page
  • Try switching from Wi‑Fi to mobile data (or vice versa)
  • Ensure your device’s GPS is turned on

Incorrect Address or Map Position

Address accuracy depends on OpenStreetMap data. If something looks off, it may be due to incomplete mapping in your area.

Nearby Places Missing

Nearby places come from OpenStreetMap. If a location isn’t listed, it may not be in their database yet.

2. Technical Support

If you’re experiencing a bug, glitch, or something isn’t working as expected, contact us with:

  • Your device type (iPhone, Android, Windows, Mac, etc.)
  • Your browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, etc.)
  • A brief description of the issue

3. Feature Requests

Have an idea to improve the site? We’d love to hear it. We’re always working on new tools and upgrades.
