Before You Email: Troubleshooting Checklist

  • Location Blocked? Ensure you have clicked the "Lock" icon in your browser address bar and set Location to "Allow".
  • Wrong Address? If on Desktop, this is normal (IP Geolocation is inaccurate). Try on Mobile for GPS precision.
  • Map Not Loading? Check your internet connection. The GPS works offline, but map tiles require data.
