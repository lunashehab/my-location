Before You Email: Common Solutions

1. "Location Denied" Error: This is a browser setting, not a site error. Click the "Lock" icon in your URL bar and toggle Location to "Allow."

2. Accuracy Issues: If you are on a desktop PC, your location is based on your ISP, which may be miles away. For precision, please use a smartphone with a GPS chip.
