Cookie Policy

Effective Date: February 16, 2026

TellMyLocation.com believes in transparency. This policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and—most importantly—how you can block them.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. They act as a "memory" for the website.

  • Session Cookies: Deleted immediately when you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device until they expire or you delete them.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for two specific purposes:

1. Preferences (Functional)
We save your language preference (e.g., English vs. Spanish) so you don't have to select it every time you visit.

2. Advertising (Monetization)
We use Google AdSense to serve ads. Google may place a cookie to understand if you have seen an ad before, preventing you from seeing the same banner repeatedly. This helps keep our tool free.

3. How to Manually Manage Cookies

You do not need to rely on our banner. You have full control at the browser level. Here is a step-by-step guide to disabling tracking:

On Chrome (Desktop)

  1. Click the three dots (⋮) in the top right corner.
  2. Go to Settings > Privacy and security.
  3. Click Third-party cookies.
  4. Select "Block all third-party cookies" for maximum privacy.

On Safari (iPhone/iOS)

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone.
  2. Scroll down and tap Safari.
  3. Under the "Privacy & Security" section, toggle Block All Cookies to ON.
  4. Note: This may log you out of other websites.

On Android (Chrome)

  1. Open Chrome app > Tap three dots > Settings.
  2. Tap Site Settings > Cookies.
  3. Toggle your preference to "Block third-party cookies."

4. Consent Mode

Our website utilizes Google Consent Mode v2. By default, we treat your status as "Denied" for all advertising cookies until you explicitly click "Accept" on our banner. We do not fire ad scripts before receiving this signal.
