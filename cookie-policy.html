Cookie Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

TellMyLocation.com ("we", "our") uses cookies to improve your experience and support our free utility. This policy explains what cookies are and how we use them.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by your web browser. They allow websites to remember your preferences (like your preferred language) or analyze how you use the site.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the site to function (e.g., remembering if you have dismissed the cookie banner).
  • Advertising Cookies (Google AdSense): We use Google AdSense to show ads. Google and its partners use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.
  • Analytics Cookies: We may use tools like Google Analytics to understand how many people visit our tool. This data is anonymized and aggregate.

3. Managing Your Choices

You have the right to accept or reject non-essential cookies. You can modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer. However, this may prevent you from taking full advantage of the website.

To reset your consent on this site specifically, please clear your browser cache for tellmylocation.com.
