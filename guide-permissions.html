How to Audit Your Browser Location Permissions

Modern browsers put you in control of your data. If you have denied location access to a site by accident, or want to revoke it for privacy, here is how to manage your settings.

Google Chrome (Desktop & Mobile)

  • To Revoke: Click the "Lock" icon ðŸ”’ in the address bar (left of the URL).
  • Find "Location" in the dropdown menu.
  • Toggle the switch to "Block" or "Ask (Default)".
  • Reload the page for changes to take effect.

Safari (iOS/iPhone)

  • Go to your iPhone Settings app.
  • Scroll down to Privacy & Security > Location Services.
  • Find Safari Websites.
  • Choose "Ask Next Time" or "Never" to reset access.

Why "Ask" is Better Than "Allow"

For maximum security, we recommend keeping your global setting on "Ask Next Time." This ensures that no website can access your GPS coordinates in the background without your explicit, momentary consent.
