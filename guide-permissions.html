How to Audit Your Browser Location Permissions

Modern browsers put you in control of your data. If you have denied location access to a site by accident, or want to revoke it for privacy, here is how to manage your settings.

Google Chrome

  • Click the "Lock" icon ðŸ”’ in the address bar.

  • Go to Settings > Privacy.
  • Tap Location Services > Safari Websites.
  • Choose "Ask Next Time" or "Never".
