About TellMyLocation

Welcome to the internet's most lightweight, privacy-centric geolocation utility. We built this tool to answer one simple question: "Where am I right now?" without the bloat, tracking, or complexity of modern map applications.

0% Data Stored
10ms Latency

Our Mission: Privacy First

In an era where location data is often sold to the highest bidder, we take a different approach. TellMyLocation.com operates on a "Zero-Knowledge" architecture. This means:

  • We do not have a database of user locations.
  • Your coordinates are processed strictly on your device (Client-Side).
  • When you close this tab, your session data is instantly wiped from memory.

The Technology

We utilize the HTML5 Geolocation API to communicate directly with your device's GPS hardware. By bypassing intermediate servers, we can offer accuracy down to 5 meters (depending on signal strength).

For weather data, we partner with Open-Meteo, an open-source weather API that aggregates data from national weather services (NOAA, DWD, MeteoFrance) to provide hyper-local forecasts without tracking your identity.

Why We Built This

This tool was created by a small team of developers and outdoor enthusiasts. We needed a way to quickly check our altitude, coordinates, and weather while hiking in low-bandwidth areas. Standard map apps were too slow to load and drained too much battery.

TellMyLocation is the solution: A Progressive Web App (PWA) that loads instantly, caches essential assets for offline use, and respects your battery life.
