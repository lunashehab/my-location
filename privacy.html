Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

TellMyLocation.com (“we”, “our”, “the site”) provides a simple, fast, and secure tool for viewing your current location and related information. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how it is used, and how your information stays safe.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Location Data (GPS Coordinates)

When you use TellMyLocation.com, your browser may request access to your device’s location. If you allow it:

  • Your latitude and longitude are processed locally on your device
  • Your coordinates are used to display your location on the map
  • Your coordinates are used to fetch optional data such as weather, altitude, and nearby places

We do NOT store, save, or log your location.

1.2 IP Address

We may display your public IP address to you using a third‑party API. This information is:

  • Shown only to you
  • Not stored by us
  • Not linked to any personal profile

1.3 Device & Browser Information

Your browser may automatically send non‑personal technical data such as browser type, operating system, and screen size. This is standard for all websites and is not used to identify you.

2. How Your Data Is Used

We use your data only to show you the information you requested, including:

  • Your coordinates
  • Your address (reverse geocoding)
  • Your weather
  • Your altitude
  • Nearby places
  • Your IP and ISP
  • Your local time zone

All processing is done in real time. We do not store or retain any personal data.

3. Third‑Party Services

TellMyLocation.com uses trusted third‑party APIs to provide accurate information:

  • OpenStreetMap / Nominatim (reverse geocoding)
  • Open‑Meteo (weather)
  • Open‑Elevation (altitude)
  • Overpass API (nearby places)
  • ipapi.co (IP lookup)
  • Leaflet.js (map display)

These services may receive your coordinates or IP address only for the purpose of returning the data you requested.

4. Cookies & Tracking

TellMyLocation.com does not use cookies, tracking pixels, analytics, or user accounts. If Google AdSense is enabled, Google may use cookies to serve personalized or non‑personalized ads.

5. Advertising (Google AdSense)

We may display ads through Google AdSense. Google may use cookies or device identifiers to serve ads, limit ad frequency, and measure performance. Google’s data use is governed by the Google Privacy & Terms page.

6. Data Storage

We do not store:

  • Your location
  • Your IP
  • Your address
  • Your weather data
  • Your altitude
  • Your nearby places
  • Any personal information

7. Children’s Privacy

TellMyLocation.com is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. The “Last updated” date will always reflect the latest version.
