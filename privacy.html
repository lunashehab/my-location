Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

At TellMyLocation.com, protecting your location privacy is our absolute priority.

1. Zero Data Storage

We do not store your location. When you use this tool, your GPS coordinates are processed entirely inside your web browser (Client-Side). Your location data is never sent to our database, never logged, and never saved. Once you close the tab, the data is gone.

2. How GPS Works Here

We use the HTML5 Geolocation API to display your coordinates on your screen. This permission is requested from your browser, and you can revoke it at any time.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Maps: We use Leaflet and OpenStreetMap (OSM) to visualize your location.
  • Weather: We send anonymous coordinates to Open-Meteo to fetch local weather data.
  • Ads: We use Google AdSense to serve ads. Google may use cookies to personalize ads based on your browsing history.

4. Cookies

We use local storage to remember your language preference. We do not track your movement across the web.
