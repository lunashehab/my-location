Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

At TellMyLocation.com, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information received and collected by TellMyLocation.com and how it is used.

1. Location Data

We do not store your location data.

This website is a "client-side" application. This means that all GPS calculations, coordinates, and address lookups happen directly in your web browser. Your precise location data is displayed to you and you alone. It is never sent to, stored on, or processed by our servers.

2. Log Files

Like many other Web sites, TellMyLocation.com makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user's movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

3. Cookies and Web Beacons

TellMyLocation.com uses "Local Storage" to remember your language preference (English, Spanish, etc.). This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to us.

DoubleClick DART Cookie

  • Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on TellMyLocation.com.
  • Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to TellMyLocation.com and other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4. Third-Party Partners

We use the following third-party services to provide functionality:

  • OpenStreetMap / Leaflet: To render the visual maps.
  • Open-Meteo: To fetch local weather data.
  • Overpass API: To locate nearby emergency services.

These services may receive your IP address or coordinates momentarily to fulfill your request (e.g., to show you the map tile for your city), but they are governed by their own respective privacy policies.

5. Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our privacy policy and agree to its terms.

6. Contact Information

If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us by email at contact@tellmylocation.com.
