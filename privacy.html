Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

At TellMyLocation.com, operated by Don System Holdings ("we", "us", or "our"), we recognize that geolocation data is among the most sensitive personal information a user can generate. This Privacy Policy outlines our strict adherence to data minimization and our "Zero-Knowledge" technical architecture.

1. No Server-Side Storage of Location Data

Our core service operates entirely on the client-side (your browser). When you grant permission for us to access your GPS:

  • The coordinates are retrieved directly by your device's operating system.
  • The data is displayed in your browser's RAM (Random Access Memory).
  • We do not transmit these coordinates to any database we control.
  • We do not store logs of where you have been.

Once you close the browser tab, the session is destroyed, and the location data is irretrievably erased from your local view.

2. Third-Party Data Processing

To provide full functionality (Weather, Maps, and Address Lookup), limited data must be sent to third-party APIs. This occurs only after you explicitly grant permission:

  • OpenStreetMap / Nominatim: Your coordinates are sent anonymously to retrieve your street address.
  • Open-Meteo: Your coordinates are sent to retrieve local weather data.
  • Google AdSense: We use Google cookies to serve ads. Google may use your general IP-based location (City/Region) to serve relevant advertisements, but they do not access the precise GPS coordinates generated by our tool unless you explicitly allow it via their specific permissions.

3. Advertising and Cookies

We use third-party vendors, including Google, which use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website or other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

4. CCPA and GDPR Compliance

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to Know: You have the right to know what data we collect. (Summary: We collect no personal data).
  • Right to Delete: Since we do not store your data, there is nothing for us to delete. It is deleted automatically when you leave the site.
  • Right to Opt-Out: You may opt out of non-essential cookies via our consent banner footer.

5. Contact for Privacy Concerns

If you have any questions regarding this policy or the technical security of our platform, please contact our Data Protection Officer at: don.odibat@gmail.com.
