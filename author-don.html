The Engineering Philosophy

Don Odibat is a systems architect focused on the intersection of Utility and Privacy. In an era where most location-based services exist primarily to harvest user data for advertising graphs, Don founded Don System Holdings to prove a different model is possible.

The Zero-Knowledge Standard:
"I believe that 'Where am I?' is a question that should be answered by your device, not a server. TellMyLocation.com was built to demonstrate that high-precision tools can exist without a backend database. If we don't collect the data, we can't lose it, leak it, or sell it."

Don oversees the entire technical stack of TellMyLocation, from the Leaflet.js mapping integration to the serverless deployment pipeline on GitHub Pages. His work focuses on optimizing Time-to-First-Fix (TTFF) for users in low-bandwidth environments.

Technical Expertise

With a background in full-stack development and network security, Don specializes in:

  • HTML5 Geolocation API: Optimizing accuracy across iOS (WebKit) and Android (Chromium) engines.
  • Progressive Web Apps (PWA): Building offline-first utilities that function without cellular data.
  • GDPR/CCPA Compliance: designing architectures that are compliant by default through data minimization.
