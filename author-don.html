About

Don Odibat is the lead engineer behind the TellMyLocation project. With a focus on "Privacy-First Architecture," Don specializes in building client-side geolocation tools that function without server-side tracking.

His work focuses on the intersection of Emergency Response Protocols and Web Geolocation APIs, ensuring that safety tools remain accessible even in low-bandwidth environments.

Philosophy

"In the digital age, 'Where am I?' is a question that shouldn't cost you your privacy. We build tools that answer that question locally, on your device, without the need for a corporate cloud."
