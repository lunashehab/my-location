Editorial Policy & Standards

At TellMyLocation.com, we operate in the "Your Money, Your Life" (YMYL) sector. Because our users rely on our tools and guides in potential emergency situations, accuracy is not just a metric—it is a safety requirement.

1. Fact-Checking Protocol

Every technical claim regarding GPS, GNSS, or cellular triangulation is verified against documentation from:

  • SOURCE The U.S. Space Force (GPS Directorate)
  • SOURCE The European Space Agency (Galileo GNSS)
  • SOURCE W3C Geolocation API Specifications

2. No "AI Slop" Guarantee

While we utilize AI tools for grammar checking and code optimization, all strategic advice, safety protocols, and technical explanations are written, reviewed, and verified by human engineers. We do not publish auto-generated safety advice.

3. Update Frequency

Technology changes fast. Browser security updates (like Chrome's deprecation of non-secure HTTP geolocation) can break tools overnight. We commit to auditing all "Guide" pages on a quarterly basis to ensure the troubleshooting steps remain relevant for the latest iOS and Android versions.

4. Conflict of Interest

TellMyLocation.com is independently funded by Don System Holdings. We do not accept payment to promote specific hardware (e.g., Garmin vs. Zoleo) in our safety guides. If a product is mentioned, it is because our engineers actually use it.

Correction Policy: If you find an error in our technical documentation, please contact the engineering team immediately at don.odibat@gmail.com. Critical safety errors are corrected within 4 hours of verification.
